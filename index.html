Urban time
in electronic motion.

Metro Sul is an electronic music project built around the urban cycle: pressure, transit, release, immersion, and return.

Its sound moves between the mechanical discipline of Chronos and the suspended intensity of Kairos — from dry machine rhythms and anxious pulses to deep nocturnal grooves, club atmospheres, blurred city lights, and emotional decompression.

More than a futuristic electronic project, Metro Sul is a sonic study of time pressure in modern life: the clock, the commute, the dance floor, the after-hours silence, and the inevitable return to the cycle.
